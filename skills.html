What We can do
SPA
We build SPAs (Salus per Aquam) that transcend water, offering a soothing experience through hydrotherapy. These SPAs combine innovation, craftsmanship, and elegance for a harmonious blend of form and function.
Waterproofing
We create aquatic sanctuaries that transcend water containment. Our waterproofing solutions offer serene hydrotherapy, blending innovation, meticulous craftsmanship, some elegance, and tranquility.

We install pool membranes. Our Renolit Alkorplan membranes are reinforced, lacquered, and non-slip. They're resistant to chlorine, perfect for heavily frequented pools. Available in 7 colors and they offer an extended warranty.
